A Little About Us

A stable, prayerful chapel community centered on the traditional Roman Rite.

Holy Family Chapel was founded in 1977 in the City of Charleston, South Carolina, and later established its permanent home in Summerville. Over the course of more than forty years, the chapel has maintained a continual presence in the Lowcountry as a self-governing traditional Roman Catholic congregation.

In September 2007, the members voted unanimously to adopt the name Holy Family Traditional Catholic Chapel, more clearly expressing the chapel’s identity as a pre-Vatican II traditional Roman Catholic community. Through the generosity of one of its early benefactors, the chapel received the residential property that now serves as its permanent chapel and rectory.

Holy Family worships exclusively according to the traditional Roman Rite, in continuity with the form codified by the Council of Trent and Pope St. Pius V. The chapel’s purpose is to provide a stable place where the perennial Catholic faith, the Traditional Latin Mass, and the sacramental life can be lived and handed on with reverence and fidelity.

We are committed to the faith that formed our ancestors, the worship that nourished the saints, and the traditions that expressed the Church’s life for centuries.

Weekday Devotions

  • All Fridays: 9:00 a.m. Mass, followed by Exposition, Adoration, and Benediction.
  • Other weekday Masses and private Masses vary. Please call ahead to confirm.

Please note: There is no Mass at Holy Family on the 3rd Sunday of each month. On those Sundays, Mass is offered at the Immaculate Heart of Mary Mission Chapel in the Murrells Inlet / Myrtle Beach area.
